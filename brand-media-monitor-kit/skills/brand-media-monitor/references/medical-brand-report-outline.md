# Medical Brand Report Outline

## Required Goal

The report is not for proving the workflow worked.

It is for helping brand, market, and management teams answer:

- what this competitor is doing
- why the content strategy works or does not work
- what should be learned
- what should be avoided
- what our next moves should be

## Recommended Sections

1. 执行摘要
2. 样本范围与证据说明
3. 内容选题分布矩阵
4. 选题策略洞察
5. 发布频率与节奏
6. 官方内容与媒体协同
7. 内容质量与信任构建
8. 用户需求与互动信号
9. 竞品优势总结
10. 竞品短板识别
11. 战略机会点
12. 可执行建议清单
13. 监测边界说明

## Writing Rules

- facts first, then interpretation, then recommendation
- every major conclusion should point to concrete examples
- avoid generic sentences such as "content is relatively rich"
- explain why a pattern matters for a growing medical brand
- separate:
  - official self-narrative
  - media pickup
  - amplified viewpoint

## Must-Have Tables

- topic distribution matrix
- monthly rhythm summary
- media role split
- action recommendation list by priority
